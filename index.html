About Me

I'm Cody, web dev and comp sci enthusiast

I am an accountant and freelance developer in Durant Oklahoma. I've worked with a variety of interesting technologies; Some of these include Go, Python, C, Java, SQL, JS, React and Django. I enjoy frontend and backend development.
I currently work at the Choctaw Nation of Oklahoma. I utilize my programming knowledge to automate a lot of my work and create specialized reports on my accounts. You can see these projects, and many of my web projects, on my GitHub account.
When I'm not working I spend my time playing diskgolf, playing guitar, running, rowing, and fishing. My favorite TV show is The Office.
